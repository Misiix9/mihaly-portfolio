# Support & Maintenance

## Warranty
- 14–30 days after launch: fixes for defects in agreed scope at no extra cost.

## Maintenance Plans (examples)
- Essentials: monthly updates, uptime checks, minor fixes.
- Pro: Essentials + performance/SEO checks, priority support.
- Custom: tailored to your stack and traffic.

## Response Targets
- Critical: 1–2 business days
- Standard: 3–5 business days

## Exclusions
- New features, third‑party outages, content entry, and major redesigns are outside maintenance unless included in your plan.
